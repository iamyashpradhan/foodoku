About Foodoku India

When the going gets tough, the tough gets Yummy! Do you ever find it overwhelming while browsing for any given meal that you want to prepare making sure, there’s a careful selection of ingredients whilst making sure you stick to your mother’s nutrition checklist? Our famous friend, the Internet can be a convoluted friend when it comes to recommending effortless healthy yet scrumptious snacking options. We’re not here to “Haw-Haw” you if you crave for a big sloppy burger and if you fail to live up to an Instagrammable feed with swanky meal posts. Nonetheless, we are crazy food nerds, and we aim to make sure we satisfy your mood-y snack attacks. We exist to celebrate food, and anyone who is a mad food lover. So anytime you find it infuriating while looking out for those drawn-out method recipes to satisfy your desi taste buds, you know where to find your peer group.
